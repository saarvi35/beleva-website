Category

Orders

How do I track my order?

Go to My Account → Orders and select your order to see live status and courier tracking ID. You’ll also receive SMS/email updates until delivery.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Edits are possible until the item is packed. Cancel the existing order and place a fresh one if the Edit option isn’t shown.

I didn’t receive an invoice. Where can I download it?

Invoices are available under My Orders → View Details → Download Invoice after dispatch. If missing, contact support with your order ID.

Category

Shipping

What are the delivery timelines?

Metro cities: 1–3 business days. Other locations: 3–7 days. Remote areas may take longer based on courier coverage.

Do you offer same-day or next-day delivery?

Available on select products and pincodes. Check the product page for Fast Delivery badge.

What should I do if my package is damaged?

Refuse delivery if severely damaged. Otherwise, record an unboxing video and raise a ticket within 48 hours for quick resolution.

Tip: Keep original packaging until your device is fully tested.

Category

Payments

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept Cards, UPI, Netbanking, Wallets (Paytm), PayPal, and Apple Pay. EMI options are shown at checkout for eligible carts.

My payment failed but money was deducted.

Most banks auto-reverse within 2–5 business days. If not credited, share the transaction reference with support for tracking.

Category

Returns & Warranty

What is the return window?

Most items are eligible for returns within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging. Health & hygiene products are non-returnable.

How do warranties work?

Manufacturer warranty periods vary by brand (typically 1 year). Keep your invoice; service centers may ask for it.

How long do refunds take?

Once the item passes QC, refunds are initiated within 24–48 hours. Bank posting times vary by method (usually 2–7 business days).

Category

Account

I can’t sign in / forgot password.

Use Forgot Password to reset via email/OTP. Check spam folder if the email isn’t received within a few minutes.

How do I change my address or phone number?

Go to Profile → Addresses to edit or add a new address. For security, some fields may need OTP verification.

Technical Support

Do you provide laptop/phone setup assistance?

Yes. Our experts can help with OS updates, data transfer, antivirus, and basic device setup through remote or in-store sessions.

How do I book a repair?

Open a support ticket with your order ID and issue summary. We’ll arrange pickup or guide you to the nearest authorized center.
